RULES
  • No Reckless Driving: No burnouts, donuts, racing, excessive revving, or dangerous maneuvers of any kind on or near the premises. Violators will be asked to leave immediately and may be reported to law enforcement.
  • Respect the Venue: No littering, vandalism, or property damage. All trash must be disposed of in designated containers.
  • Noise Control: Keep music and exhaust noise at a reasonable level to avoid disturbing local businesses and residents.
  • Park only in designated areas: Do not block fire lanes, driveways, entrances, or emergency access routes.
  • Substances & Alcohol: Absolutely no alcohol, drugs, or illegal substances on-site. Individuals under the influence will be removed immediately.
  • Behavior Expectations: Treat all participants, spectators, and property with respect. No harassment, fighting, or disorderly conduct will be tolerated.
  • Spectator Safety: Stay in designated spectator areas. Do not stand in active traffic lanes or near moving vehicles. Leave Weapons and valuables at home. DCC & its Venue(s) are not responsible for stolen, lost, or damaged property.
  • Compliance with Laws: All local, state, and federal laws (traffic, noise, trespass, etc.) must be obeyed. Vehicles must be street legal if operated on public roads.
